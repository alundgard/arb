Stinkdigital

  • A global company with a local feel.

    We launched in London in 2009 with a small team. Today, we’re one of the world’s most awarded digital companies and also call New York, Los Angeles, Paris and Berlin home, with a few more offices on the way.

  • Work that invites active attention.

    The internet is an attention economy, but not all attention is created equal. That’s why we invent, design and build digital campaigns and experiences that people truly *want* to spend time with. We call this “active attention” and we attract it with work that’s beautiful, entertaining, useful, and anything but boring.

  • We get up in the morning to make things.

    For us, craft isn’t just a set of skills; it’s a philosophy that informs everything we do. Making things isn’t the end of our process, it *is* our process.

  • Where clients, culture and craft matter most.

    We have fun because we love what we do. We want our clients to enjoy working with us as much as we enjoy solving the challenges they bring us.

  • Honor the details.

    The tiniest things can make the biggest difference. Whether it’s presentations, prototypes, insights, designs or code, we sweat the small details because that’s often where the magic is.

Services

  • Our core services span four key disciplines:

  • Strategy is the foundation of every project. It can be rooted in culture, channels, business or audience insights; our only requirement is that it has to be actionable.

  • Our Creative department invents, design and build digital campaigns and experiences with an equal focus on concept and execution.

  • Whether it’s original video, photography, copy, or music, our nimble approach and close-knit network of partners lets us deliver incredible Content at a range of budgets.

  • Our Technology team delivers beautiful, efficient code, as well as some of our smartest ideas.

The Network

One of the world's most diverse and awarded creative networks.

Comprised of Stinkfilm, Stinkdigital and Stinkstudios, the Stink Group creates film, interactive and original content for clients across advertising, entertainment, music and the arts.

Television commercials, music videos and feature films.

With ten offices and some of the world's top directorial talent, Stinkfilm has been at the forefront of the commercials business since the early 2000s.

Digital strategy, creative and execution.

With five offices and a staff of over one hundred creatives, designers, developers and strategists, Stinkdigital is one of the world’s most awarded digital studios.

Content creation and strategy.

Launched in 2015, Stinkstudios is a modern content studio tailored to meet the demand for high volume, quick turnaround original content.
