Shared Web Browsing for Accessibility

You must be using Google Chrome to complete this HIT.

If you ignore this requirement, you will not be compensated.

Overview:

  • You will collaborate with a blind/low-vision participant in navigating and retrieving specific information from a shared webpage.
  • You will both see the same webpage content.
  • You will interact with the participant via a chat panel.
  • You can propose actions to take on the webpage (such as clicking a button) but the participant will accept or reject them.
 Accept the HIT to do a tutorial, after which you will be forwarded to a waiting page before beginning the task. You will be paid for doing both the tutorial and the task.
